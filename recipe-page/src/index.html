Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy adn quick dish, perfect for meal. this classic omelette combines besten eggs cooked to perdection, optionally filled with your choise of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large agss
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butther or oil
  • Optional fillings cheee, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Reat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pich of salt and papper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egss. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (Optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one halt of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it anto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt ad pepper if needed
